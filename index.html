SCAMRADAR AU

Real-time scam intelligence for Australians

Australians lost $2.18 billion to scams in 2025. Official warnings take days to reach the public. Scammers move in hours.

ScamRadar AU closes that gap — aggregating scam alerts from official sources and community reports, in real time, free for everyone.

How It Works

  • We monitor Scamwatch, ACCC, ASIC, AFP and Australian news
  • The feed updates automatically every day
  • Community members submit scams they've encountered
  • Verified patterns get published as alerts to warn others
